Taking advantage of our experience and influence in the industry, we launched the brand with the purpose of creating and organizing our own events bri nging together the most important figures in the branches of entertainment.

Our events aim to promote different sports and entertainment activities, creating a unique audiovisual experience. We aim to unite the best of both worlds in one place.

In 2022, we are dedicated to holding events exclusively for Padel. We have the participation of Content creators such as Coscu, Yeyito de Gregorio, Markito Navaja and Joaco Lopez among others.

During 2022 we have the support of brands such as RedBull, HEAD, Strong Dreams, Hashi and Loot among others.

2 Individual editions were held

Throughout the year, two Individual editions were held, of which the winning team of each reached the final. This final was held at the Argentina Game Show, the largest entertainment event in Latin America.

Within the event, held at the Costa Salguero Center in the city of Buenos Aires, we had our own Padel court in which, apart from the final, several exhibition matches were held, highlighting figures such as Coscu or the historic Tennis player, Guillermo Coria.
